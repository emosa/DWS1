Welcome to Rest. Puerta al SoL

At Puerta's al SoL restaurant in Salinas, you'll indulge fresh seafood sitting at the water's edge on the South Coast of Puerto Rico.

With three levels of dining, open-air views of the sea for casual dining, an authentic wine cellar for very intimate parties and two air-conditioned rooms for elegant dining, you'll experience the best of the Puetorrican dishes.

To lift the spirits we also offer the "7" Powers seafood soup with octopus, conch, lobster and shrimp in fish stock and land crab gazpacho. We also make a variety of seafood specialties, steaks, mofongos, seafood paella, and grilled lobster.

The great taste and quality of our dishes and recommendations of our customers, have propelled the continued growth of our restaurant.
